About Us

Welcome To Our Website

Zero Hunger Initiative is a comprehensive campaign, considered to be at the frontier of work in the eradication of hunger and securing right to food for all citizens. Our goal is to create a flow that supports any one's desire for food and we are convinced this can be done with our proven model of utilizing three forces-collaboration, technology & community in practice.

Our Story

The Zero Hunger Plan got its start as a global commitment to feed all of the people on earth who are under-nourished or food insecure. Hunger is a burning issue around the world and even after all global efforts still we are facing this thing.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where everyone has access to sufficient nutritious food. We envision future where communities are empowered to develop sustainable food systems, and where collaboration and partnerships among governments, NGOs,and individuals are the norm.
